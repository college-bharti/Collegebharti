About Sainath University

Sai Nath University is a private university located in Ranchi. The university was established in 2012 under the Sai Nath University Act passed by the Jharkhand State Legislature and is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956.

The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and doctoral programs across multiple academic disciplines. These include fields such as Engineering, Management, Law, Pharmacy, Nursing, Agriculture, Education, Social Sciences, Travel & Tourism, Hospitality, and Communication.

Accreditation

Several programs are approved by professional bodies such as the Bar Council of India, Pharmacy Council of India, National Council for Teacher Education, and Indian Nursing Council, depending on the course.

Career guidance and placement support

The university has a placement cell that assists students with training and recruitment opportunities through campus placement activities and industry connections.

Modern academic infrastructure

The campus provides facilities such as laboratories, libraries, computer labs, and other learning resources to support academic and practical education.
